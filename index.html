Welcome to my website

The pages of this website were designed to teach you how to use social media apps as a way of teaching and learning. Students today are familiar with the apps Instagram, Youtube, Pinterest, Whatsapp and Twitter, some even having an personal account on these apps. For this reason I believe using them for education would be in great interest of the students and a great way to be interactive while learning. These apps were not designed with the intent of using them for education but they have great features that are very useful for both learning and teaching. By continuing to read the information provided by my website I hope to teach you how to use these apps in your lessons
